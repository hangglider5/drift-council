# Drift Council — 45–55 Second Video Script

Record the real expanded Reddit post at 1920×1080 and 30 fps. Keep the cursor deliberate, cut all loading and dead time, use only app sound-free footage, and burn in the exact captions below. Export an H.264 MP4 and verify its public or unlisted URL in a signed-out browser.

| Time   | Exact caption                                                                             | Screen action                                                                                                            |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 0–5s   | `One gust each. One voyage together.`                                                     | Open on the Drift Council inline post, then enter the expanded voyage.                                                   |
| 5–15s  | `A new shared wind field appears every UTC day.`                                          | Hold on the full 6×6 field, route, storms, beacon, contribution count, and UTC countdown.                                |
| 15–27s | `Choose one tile and one direction. Preview the consequence before you commit.`           | Select a clear grid cell, choose one cardinal direction, and let the cyan preview route settle before moving the cursor. |
| 27–37s | `Devvit Redis commits one immutable gust per redditor, then everyone sees the new route.` | Click Commit once, show the submitting state, then show the updated contribution count and authoritative route.          |
| 37–47s | `Reach the beacon—or watch the council drift into the squall.`                            | Replay the route from its origin and hold on the outcome copy.                                                           |
| 47–55s | `Drift Council. A Reddit community experiment built with Devvit Web and Phaser.`          | End on the luminous route and amber beacon with the product title visible.                                               |

## Capture checklist

- Use the stable public demo post: https://www.reddit.com/r/driftcouncildemo/comments/1ux0n8c/drift_council_one_gust_each_one_voyage_together/
- Keep the final duration between 45 and 55 seconds; target 52 seconds.
- Record a fresh UTC-day contribution or use a clearly isolated capture subreddit. Never describe fixture gusts as real users.
- Make every caption readable on a 1280-pixel-wide Devpost embed.
- Do not expose Reddit notifications, account details, OAuth URLs, terminal output, or developer tokens.
- Export H.264 MP4 at 1080p/30 fps.
- Upload to a Devpost-supported public or unlisted host.
- Verify playback, captions, and the final URL in a signed-out browser before submission.
